What is a Joint Display?

A joint display is a table or visual that presents quantitative and qualitative data side-by-side in a single integrated format. It's the hallmark of quality mixed-methods research because it shows you're truly integrating, not just reporting methods separately. Joint displays help you identify convergence, divergence, and generate meta-inferences.
